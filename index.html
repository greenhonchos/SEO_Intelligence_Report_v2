GH Insight
Quality of traffic is compounding — but CTR decay is masking impressions growth.

Organic revenue grew +35.5% on only +15.9% session growth — the strongest quality-of-traffic improvement in 6 months. Two structural drivers: (1) Co-ord Sets jumped from rank 20 → 8 on cotton co ords (480 vol) and cotton co ord set (6,600 vol) unlocking high-AOV demand. (2) Returning organic users now convert at 3.6% vs 1.5% for new — retention loops are kicking in.

Risk: Site-wide CTR is down -10.5% despite +9.3% impressions — meta-title decay on Tops-Shirts collection and the Crop-Top blog is leaking roughly 4,800 monthly clicks. Paid is overpaying for the brand keyword "babli" (₹15.3K spend, 6.0 ROAS, organic rank 1) — ~₹14.2L annualised savings available if reallocated.
